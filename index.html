The Neighbour Within

The Hidden History of Concentration Camps in Italy

The discrimination faced by Jews in Italy and the 11 Concentration Camps on Italian soil during World War II remain vastly unacknowledged, leaving many in the Italian Jewish communities struggling to keep their history of persecution alive as their memories at times run completely contrary to the official narrative now prevalent in Italian society.
